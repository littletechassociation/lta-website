Sign the launch letters

LTA isn't tiptoeing into Washington. The day we go public, we're sending two letters — one to the Trump Administration, one to Congress — with every member's name attached. We want that list long enough that nobody can pretend Little Tech doesn't have a seat at the table.

Add us to the AI Access Letter to the Administration read the full letter → Calls on the Administration to keep frontier AI open to startups — and to scrap the closed-door vetting regime that routes the most powerful models to the biggest incumbents and shuts the rest of us out.
Add us to the Competition Letter to Congress read the full letter → Tells Congress to stop stalling on the reforms that would level the field — ending the self-preferencing, app-store gatekeeping, and customer lock-in that kill startups before they can scale.
